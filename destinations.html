Paris is the capital and most populous city of France. Situated on the Seine River, in the north of the country, it is in the centre of the Île-de-France region, also known as the région parisienne, "Paris Region".

Nuwara Eliya, known as Sri Lanka's "Little England," is a hill station famous for its colonial architecture, cool climate, and tea plantations

Kandy, Sri Lanka, is famous for the Temple of the Tooth Relic, a sacred Buddhist site housing a tooth believed to belong to the Buddha
