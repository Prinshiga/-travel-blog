About Me Travel Stories

Paris, France

Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light" and is famous for its rich history, art, fashion, and romance.

Highlights:

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower
  • Explore the Louvre Museum
  • Walk along the Seine River

Nuwara Eliya, Sri Lanka

Nuwara Eliya, often called "Little England," is a peaceful hill station surrounded by tea plantations, waterfalls, and colonial charm.

Highlights:

  • Tour tea plantations & factories
  • Visit Gregory Lake
  • Explore Hakgala Botanical Garden

Kandy, Sri Lanka

Kandy is the cultural capital of Sri Lanka, home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic and surrounded by lush green hills.

Highlights:

  • Temple of the Tooth Relic
  • Royal Botanical Gardens
  • Kandy Lake Walk

London, United Kingdom

London is a vibrant global city known for its historic landmarks, cultural diversity, and modern attractions.

Highlights:

  • Big Ben & Houses of Parliament
  • London Eye
  • British Museum
